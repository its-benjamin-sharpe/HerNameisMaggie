Another Sleepless Night Over Yourself

You say it’s another sleepless night over me. I say it’s another sleepless night over yourself.

You clearly need something I cannot offer. I mean that as sincerely as one could after being accused of such bizarre shit...

I even went to the furthest extent and told you that if you had lied or kept something from me, I could understand...

Let me rephrase something—because you probably never saw the apartment as a home...

Everything was off.

I had never asked a question I already knew the answer to just to see if you would lie...

Listen, nobody—and I mean nobody—who truly loves someone sits back and watches them go through that...

By God, it is me.

I’m the fool who is so hopelessly desperate to be loved, to be cared for...

And yet, if you really believed the things you accused me of, you never would have come back...

Stop pretending.

Stop trying to convince me—hell, stop trying to convince yourself...

You’ve always been a pro at lying.

And I knew that from the moment you were telling your mother you were at Autumn’s house...

I pray one day, someone comes along that you actually want to act right for...

But you and I both know—you won’t read this far.

And if you do, you won’t really hear it.
